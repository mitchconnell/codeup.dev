Park FAQ

Acadia

  • Covers most of Mount Desert Island
  • Features the tallest mountain on the Atlantic coast
  • Has freshwater estuary
  • Has intertidal habitats

American Samoa

  • It is the southernmost national park is on three Samoan islands
  • Protects coral reefs
  • It is home to flying foxes
  • It is home to 900 species of fish

Arches

  • This site features more than 2,000 natural sandstone arches
  • Millions of years of erosion have led to these structures
  • The arid ground has life-sustaining soil
  • Other geologic formations are stone columns and towers
